About DeepInSkin

Built in the lab.
Designed for industry.

DeepInSkin is a spin-off from the University of Bologna's Biomedical Science, Technologies and Nanobiotechnology Lab and the IRCCS Rizzoli Orthopaedic Institute.

We provide advanced efficacy testing services for cosmetic active ingredients through MOSAIC, our patented platform for 3D spheroid generation, live imaging, and biological analysis.

Unlike traditional approaches, MOSAIC enables non-destructive, time-lapse 3D evaluation of active ingredients, allowing us to monitor biological responses over time while preserving tissue integrity.

Our team has extensive expertise in exosome research, supported by scientific publications and the development of innovative proprietary testing protocols specifically designed for next-generation cosmetic actives.

By combining advanced 3D biology, live imaging, and quantitative analysis, we help cosmetic brands, ingredient manufacturers, and R&D laboratories generate robust efficacy data, accelerate product development, and strengthen scientific claim substantiation.
